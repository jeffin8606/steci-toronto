About Us

Welcome to the STECI Toronto Parish!

We are a Christ-centered, Bible-based community committed to worship, fellowship, and service. Founded in the tradition of the St. Thomas Evangelical Church of India, we aim to nurture faith, build families, and serve our city. Through our regular worship services, youth engagement, Sunday School, and missions, we work together to live out the message of the Gospel.
